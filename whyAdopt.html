See how interesting cats are! See how cute they are! How can you resist!?!

But seriously, cats are both capable of being wonderful companions and are in need of loving homes. ADOPT, don't SHOP. There are an estimated 60-100 million homeless cats in the U.S.A alone. Additionally, cats can even improve your mental health. If you have been feeling lonely and can handle the responsiblity, cats are great companions who need less daily investment than dogs who need long walks.
