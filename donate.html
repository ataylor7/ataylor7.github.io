Help a cat in need

Of about 3.2 million cats who enter shelters each year, 860k are euthanized; that's almost 1 of every 4 cats to enter a shelter.

This is not because the people running shelters hate animals, but because supplies, space, caretakers, and medical work require money, money, money. If you can't adopt, or already have but still want to help, donate to some of your local shelters now to help a cat in need.
