Welcome to Alyssa Taylor's Final Project!

This site is intended to help you adopt a pet cat (or two or three or-) of your very own!

The navigation bar at the top contains different pages that will assist you in this process.

HOME is here!

WHY ADOPT will convince you of why adopting a cat is a wonderful idea that will bring you fulfillment.

BEFORE ADOPING will help you make sure you know what you are getting into.

ADOPT will allow you to actually see shelters near you!

DONATE theoretically lets you donate to the site, who would then donate to different shelters. Please do not actually do this.

The next icon (the eye) lets you toggle light mode versus dark mode!

Signing in and account info do not work, but to have a pleasant looking top navigation bar, I left those in!
